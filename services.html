NSS

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is an Indian government-sponsored public service programme conducted by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports. NSS is a voluntary association of students in colleges, universities and at higher secondary schools working for strengthening a campus-community linkage. The Institute of Technology offers NSS programme for the students. A strong and vibrant 400 plus students NSS unit organises a number of activities such as teaching children at the adopted villages of Miroli and Motipura, organising residential camps, awareness drives on societal issues, mentoring and teaching construction workers children, cleanliness drives and many more.

LIBRARY

Information is an essential input to national development. The modern library has great responsibility in harnessing and utilization of information. The library and information centers strive for high quality of library services which emphasis the actual delivery of information rather than actual delivery of documents for meeting their generic as well as specific needs of the users. The Central Library follows the open access system, encouraging the user to browse freely in stock area. Standard Text books and books by authors of repute in all fields taught at the college are stocked. The college follows a policy of buying multiples copies of many of these books to make them available to a large number of students. The College library has 63830 books, 5603 reference titles and 69 journals that cater to all the eight branches of UG and PG courses. All major newspapers, news magazines and books of general interest are also made available.
